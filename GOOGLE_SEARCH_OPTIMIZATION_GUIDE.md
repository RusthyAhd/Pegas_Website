## üîç HOW GOOGLE AUTOCOMPLETE WORKS

Google search autocomplete suggestions appear based on:

1. **Search Volume** (30% weight)
   - How many people search for "pegas"
   - Needs thousands of searches per month

2. **Brand Recognition** (25% weight)
   - How well-known your brand is
   - Media mentions, news coverage

3. **Direct Traffic** (20% weight)
   - How many people type "pegas.lk" directly in browser
   - Shows brand loyalty

4. **User Behavior** (15% weight)
   - Search history patterns
   - Clicking your result when searching "pegas"

5. **Domain Authority** (10% weight)
   - Backlinks from quality websites
   - Domain age (you're new - established 2024)

## üì± BROWSER AUTOCOMPLETE vs GOOGLE SEARCH AUTOCOMPLETE

**Important distinction:**

1. **Browser address bar autocomplete** (appears in Chrome/Safari URL bar)
   - Based on user's browsing history
   - Appears faster (immediate if user visited your site)
   - This will work once people visit pegas.lk

2. **Google search autocomplete** (appears in google.com search box)
   - Based on global search patterns
   - Takes 6-12+ months to appear
   - Requires significant brand awareness

**Your users might see "pegas.lk" in browser autocomplete after visiting once, but Google search autocomplete needs much more time and effort.**
