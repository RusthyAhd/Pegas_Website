## Critical Steps YOU MUST Take (To Get Your Site Indexed) üìã

### Step 1: Submit to Google Search Console ‚≠ê MOST IMPORTANT

1. **Go to Google Search Console**
   - Visit: https://search.google.com/search-console/
   - Sign in with your Google account

2. **Add Your Property**
   - Click "Add Property"
   - Select "URL prefix"
   - Enter: `https://pegas.lk`
   - Click "Continue"

3. **Verify Ownership** (Choose ONE method):
   
   **Method A: HTML File Upload** (Easiest)
   - Download the verification file Google provides
   - Upload it to your website root directory
   - Click "Verify"
   
   **Method B: Meta Tag** (Already prepared)
   - Copy the verification code Google gives you
   - Open your `index.html`
   - Find this line: `<meta name="google-site-verification" content="" />`
   - Paste your code between the quotes
   - Upload to your server
   - Click "Verify" in Search Console

4. **Submit Your Sitemap**
   - After verification, click "Sitemaps" in left menu
   - Enter: `sitemap.xml`
   - Click "Submit"

### Step 2: Request Indexing

1. In Google Search Console:
   - Click "URL Inspection" at the top
   - Enter: `https://pegas.lk`
   - Click "Test Live URL"
   - Click "Request Indexing"
   - Repeat for important pages

## Troubleshooting Common Issues üîß

### Problem: "Site not appearing after 1 week"
**Solution:**
- Check Google Search Console for crawl errors
- Verify sitemap was successfully submitted
- Request indexing again
- Ensure robots.txt is not blocking

### Problem: "Appearing but not first"
**Solution:**
- Build more quality backlinks
- Increase brand mentions online
- Create Google Business Profile
- Get reviews and ratings
- Create more content with "Pegas" keyword

### Problem: "Favicon not showing"
**Solution:**
- Clear browser cache (Cmd+Shift+R on Mac)
- Wait 24-48 hours for Google to recrawl
- Check favicon is accessible: https://pegas.lk/favicon_image.png
- Ensure favicon_image.png is at least 512x512 pixels
